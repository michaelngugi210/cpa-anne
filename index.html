Who We Are

Nduta Mwangi & Associates is a registered firm of Certified Public Accountants based in Mombasa, Kenya. Our mission is to empower businesses, organizations, and professionals with dependable financial insights and compliant accounting practices.

Led by CPA Anne (Nduta) Mwangi, CPAK, a finance and accounting expert with extensive experience in auditing, IPSAS reporting, taxation, and risk management, we bring both technical expertise and personal integrity to every client relationship.

  • Integrity: We uphold professional ethics in every engagement.
  • Accuracy: Every figure counts.
  • Transparency: Clear communication builds lasting trust.
  • Excellence: We don’t just meet standards — we exceed them.
